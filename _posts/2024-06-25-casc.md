The Center for Applied Scientific Computing ([CASC](https://computing.llnl.gov/casc)) has published a [new issue of its newsletter](https://computing.llnl.gov/casc/newsletter/vol-14) highlighting contributions to the [Exascale Computing Project](https://www.exascaleproject.org/). Included in the issue are the following articles, which feature many open source projects:

- [From the Director](https://computing.llnl.gov/casc/newsletter/vol-14#Director)
- [EQSIM: Earthquake Simulation Using the SW4 Code](https://computing.llnl.gov/casc/newsletter/vol-14#Collab)
- [zfp: Fast and Accurate Data Compression for Modern Supercomputing Applications](https://computing.llnl.gov/casc/newsletter/vol-14#Impact)
- [UMap and Metall: ECP Products Under the Argo and SICM Projects](https://computing.llnl.gov/casc/newsletter/vol-14#Discipline)
- [ExaLearn: Co-design Center for Exascale Machine Learning Technologies](https://computing.llnl.gov/casc/newsletter/vol-14#Machine)
